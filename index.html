Projects

4.O.4

4.0.4 is a conceptual AI-driven project that visualizes the intricacies of OCD through 404 meticulously layered rules, exacting symmetry, and carefully curated palettes that symbolize compulsions. The highlight piece, Neural Pathways, captures the cyclical nature of OCD with algorithmic precision, offering a striking visual narrative that sparks awareness and conversations on mental health.

NIJI

Niji redefines simplicity by fusing AI with minimal design, typography, and color theory. The platform blends over five distinct minimalistic art styles with AI-assisted composition tools, guiding artists toward refined, intentional creations while showcasing the depth of simplicity.

ZYTE

Zyte is a digital art platform that transforms raw binary data into dynamic visual experiences. Using code-driven generative design, it explores the intersection of technology and creativity, allowing users to interact with and visualize data through binary patterns.

Örnek

Örnek is a design initiative that merges cultural storytelling with advanced 3D modeling techniques to create intricate ornaments and jewelry. Using Blender and Tinkercad, the project employs symmetry, parametric modeling, typography, and 3D printing to craft wearable art that honors tradition while embracing modern design.

JANAIQAI

Janaiqai Comics is a project dedicated to shedding light on abuse in Central Asia through storytelling. The series presents real-life survivor stories in the form of comics, using compelling characters and impactful narratives to raise awareness about child violence.

LueurDeMuse

LueurDeMuse (Glow of the Muse) is a non-profit revolutionizing the digital art world by empowering women in 3D creative art, creative development, and the fusion of computer science with art. We break the stereotype that the tech and CS fields are male-dominated, creating a space where women can thrive as both artists and innovators.
